My name is Bartłomiej Wójtowicz, but you can call me Bart for short. I am currently the CTO at Selleo Labs, where we support our clients with business and software development. As a passionate software engineer, I am always eager to learn new things and expand my knowledge. In my free time, I enjoy various hobbies such as recording sounds of nature, learn-playing the piano, and honing my drawing skills. Animation enthusiast, both films and shows. While I occasionally play video games, my true fascination lies in understanding the low-level magic that happens under the hood. I am particularly interested in the areas of rendering, architecture, game design, AI, physics, and math. Together with my cousin, we run a small indie game development studio called Soft Kiwi Games, where we bring our passion for gaming and software development to life.
